About Us

Leaf Plant is dedicated to bringing fresh and safe, locally grown produce to families, communities, and group-buys across Singapore. Our mission is to ensure that every household has access to affordable, pesticide-free, and responsibly harvested vegetables.

We work closely with local farmers, adopting sustainable practices that minimise waste and preserve soil health. From seed to harvest, every step is guided by our commitment to quality and care.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Leaf Plant, sustainability is at the heart of everything we do. From responsible harvesting to reducing waste, we ensure that every vegetable is grown with care for both people and the environment.
